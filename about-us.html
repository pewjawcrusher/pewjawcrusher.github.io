About Us

Shanghai Shibang Machinery Co., Ltd. is a high-technology minerals processing company, Professional manufacturer of mining crushers and industrial grinding mills, located in beautiful Pudong New District, with factory area of more than 90,000 square meters.Dating from a solid foundation over 30 years, Shanghai Shibang Machinery Corporation(sbm) has grown to be the largest manufacturers in mining crushers and industrial grinding mills in China.Till now, we have formed a whole production chain with main products: crushing machinery, Grinding machinery, and auxiliary products: vibrating screen, vibrating feeder and other associated equipments. Products cover more than 20 models of 3 major series: mill series, crushing series, and sand making series. Advanced technology content, high reliability, low operation and maintenance cost have enabled our products to take the learock position in the field.

Shibang has cultivated a professional technology and after-sales service team, and successfully achieved ISO9001：2000 Quality Management Certificate and EU CE Certificate.The company features advanced CAD and advanced Solidworks 3D mechanical design, which ensures the quality of design, development, manufacturing and after-sale service.Our products have been exported to more than 130 countries and are widely used in global mining and construction industry. Being innovative and perfect is our incessant pursuit, and also our vigor source of energy and harmony.
