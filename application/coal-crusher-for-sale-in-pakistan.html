Coal Crusher For Sale in Pakistan

Pakistan Coal

Coal plays an important role in modern industry. The coal is processed and crushed to increase the efficiency and protect environment. In order to use coal more rationally, efficiently and effectively, the coal need to be processed and crushed into different sizes, 0 ~ 25 mm particle size for coal gas stove, 25 ~ 8 mm for steam locomotive, 25 ~ 6 mm for the industrial furnace, and coal power plant requires grain coal of 0 ~ 25mm.

Pakistan is endowed with significant mineral resources and emerging as a very promising area for prospecting/exploration of mineral deposits. Currently about 52 minerals are under exploitation although on small scale. The major production is of coal, rock salt and other industrial and construction minerals.

Coal Crusher

SBM is professional in producing crushers,grinding mill, feeding and screening machines, conveyors and laundry equipments, ore crushing plant, rock quarry crusher, mineral processing plants etc.coal jaw crusher is the most common crusher equipment in coal crusher plant and used as primary coal crusher. it can crush massive coal into small pieces. impact crusher is another common crusher used in coal crushing process. it is mainly used in primary or secondary crushing process.

The raw stone materials will be uniformly sent to the primary crusher for coarse crushing. And then the coarse stone materials will be fed into the impact crusher by the belt conveyor to proceed the secondary crushing. And the fine stone materials will be sent to the vibrating screen to produce various stone specifications. the particle sizes which have not met the standard will receive repeated crushing.

Coal Mine Equipment

The coal mining is surface open-pit mining. its crushing plant consists of jaw crusher, two cone crushers, vibrating feeder, vibrating screen and belt conveyor. the jaw crusher is 900*1200 as primary coal crusher to crush big coal block excavated from coal deposit. the two cone crushers are as secondary crusher to crush coal smaller. the vibrating feeder is feeding coal into crusher and vibrating screen is sieving crushed coal into several size degrees.
