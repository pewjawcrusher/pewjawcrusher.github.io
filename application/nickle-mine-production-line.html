Nickle Ore Crushing Process

Typically, nickel ore crusher refers to the crusher used in the crushing process of nickel ore. As we know, nickel ore is very hard, so it takes a longer time for crushing. In the nickel processing, the jaw crushers are used in the coarse crushing, and cone crushers are used in the secondary or fine crushing. Usually, according to the quarry conditions, mobile crushers are also used in the nickel quarrying. After the two stages of nickel ore crushing, large nickel rock whose diameter is approximately 1.5 meters has changed into nickel ore particle, and the size of nickel ore particles can achieve to 25mm or less. Obviously, it can fully meet the technical requirement of open-pit nickel processing.The ball mill is a key piece of equipment for grinding crushed materials, and it is widely used in production lines for powders such as cement, silicates, refractory material, fertilizer, glass ceramics, etc. as well as non-ferrous metals. The ball mill can grind various ores and other materials either wet or dry. The grinding of nickel ore is suitable for using ball mill, what is more, the hollow axis of SBM ball mill adopts the cast steel, which can make our ball mill run smooth and reliable when processing the nickel ore.
