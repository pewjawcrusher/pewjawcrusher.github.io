Stone Crusher

Stone crushers machine are utilised to break down stones. They have applications in a number of different industries, like mining. Stone crusher are primarily made use of in building activities which includes Constructing, Roads,and Bridges etc.stone crushers come in two big sorts, principal and secondary. Secondary crushers work with smaller pieces of stone that have already been broken down from their original size.

Hammer crusher:major or secondary stone crushers .It is employed for crushing raw materials that have compressive strength much less than 150MPa for instance limestone, marl, sandstone, gypsum, coal, etc. The single stage hammer crusher can crush the ore block at the stope into the granularity needed at a time. Stone Cone Crusher

Fitting into secondary crushing circuits that used to require larger crushers, SBM stone cone crusher can reduce the capital requirements for plant expansion projects. Accepting extra coarse feed, the crushing machine allows primary crushers to operate at larger settings and at greater capacities, providing opportunities to increase plant production with minimum investment.

Stone cone crushers are the most commonly used crusher type for secondary and tertiary crushing stages in both the rock and the mining industry. Because of the large number of active operating crushers in the world, a very strong global common incentive is to maximize performance and minimize energy consumption and liner wear rate.In order to be able to design and create more application specific cone crushers, optimized towards specific conditions and constraints, better evaluation tools are needed in the design process.
