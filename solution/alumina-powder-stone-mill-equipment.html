Alumina Powder Stone Mill Equipment

Specialized unit intended for pulverizing materials in the extreme fineness range. The principle of integral air classification, originally developed by Raymond, has been applied with outstanding success to this mill and as a result a great number of materials can be produced in the lower micron sizes with a really minimum of oversize. Alumina Raymond vertical mill has high output and it will bring higher profits to customers.The feed Alumina materials enters the Raymond vertical mill through the side mounted variable speed screw feeder between the hammers and the whizzer classifier. The airflow enters below the grinding chamber and carries pulverized material vertically, combining it with newly introduced feed, to the classifier where any acceptable material is removed from the mixture prior to getting ground.

Alumina Ore Raymond Mill Application

Raymond mill is mainly applied to the powder processing of mineral products in industries of metallurgy, construction, chemistry, and mining etc. It can produce powder from more than 280 kinds of non-flammable and non-explosive mineral materials with Mohs hardness below 7 and humidity below 6%, such as barite, limestone, quartz, calcite, granite, porcelain clay, basalt, gypsum etc. The final size is between 40 meshes and 600meshes.The granularity of the end products can be adjusted from 30 to 325 meshes.
