Crushing And Screening Plant – Crushing Equipment

Now in the industrial application of broken equipment variety, also have a variety of its classification method. Broken equipment can be according to the working principle and structural characteristics are divided into: jaw crusher, cone crusher, roll crusher, impact crusher and grinding mill, etc.

  • 1. Jaw Crusher Jaw crusher crushing work is by moving jaw cycle pressure to the fixed jaw plate, will be between jaw material crushing. According to the moving jaw movement trajectory, can be divided into simple pendulum jaw crusher with swing jaw crusher complex. Jaw crusher commonly known as “tiger”, is one of the crusher has a long history, still is the most effective equipment broken hard materials.
  • 2. cone crusher cone crusher is with the help of the rotary pendulum movement conical surface, near the fixed cone cycle, clip in two cone products material is extruded and bending and crushing. It can be divided into used in coarse crushing gyratory crusher and used to finely cone crusher.
  • 3. Impact crusher Impact crusher is a kind of the use of impact energy to crushing material crushing machinery. Machine work, under the drive of the motor, the rotor high-speed rotation, the material into the plate hammer function area, and the rotor plate hammer impact broken, and then again to counter device is broken, and then back to the board from the back plate hammer function area is broken again, this process is repeated, material from big to small back into one, two, three cavity repeated broken, until the material being broken to the required size, is being exhausted from port discharge. Adjust the clearance between the back frame and rotor can achieve the purpose of the material discharging granularity and material to change shape.
  • 4. VSI Crusher VSI crusher is a high speed rotating rotor, with the impact hammer above, after the material into the crusher, the high-speed rotating hammer or energy from the high speed rotating rotor, the high-speed into the crusher wall or AD hoc made by smash.

Mix particles of different size material, through a single layer or multilayer sieve And divided into a number of different granularity levels of the process is called Screening. City.there are equipped with fixed screen sieve, inertia vibrating screen, since the fixed center shaker, heavy vibrating screen energy-saving ball mill, ball mill equipment resonance screen, linear vibrating screen.

  • 1. Fixed screen fixed screen is composed of parallel arrangement of steel or steel bar, steel bar and steel bar called article, article, borrow rail link together, riffle the gaps between the size is the mesh size. Fixed screen is divided in two types: the grizzly and grizzly.
  • 2. The inertia vibrating screen vibrating screen is widely used in industry of a sieve, wide application range, suitable for medium and finely in advance and check the sieve. According to the trajectory characteristics of sieve box can be divided into circular movement vibrating screen and straight vibrating screen. The former consists of single axle inertia vibrating screen, since the fixed center shaker and heavy vibrating screen; The latter including double-axis inertia vibrating screen and resonance screen, press the screen layer can also be divided into two categories, single screen and double deck screen.
  • 3. Since the fixed center shaker since the centering of the vibrating screen is suitable for large and medium-sized plant screening medium and fine grained material. Usually for hanging screen, but also has a type screen.
  • 4. The principle of heavy vibrating screen heavy vibrating screen and set the center vibrating screen are similar, but not eccentric vibrator of spindle, the pulley eccentric shaft hole to achieve the goal of self centering during operation. Heavy vibrating screen structure is strong enough to withstand a larger impact load, suitable for screening large degree, the relative density of the material, the largest feed up to 350 mm. Is mainly used for broken in its preliminary screening, in addition, the water cut, high silt content of the material, can be in advance before the crusher, screening and washing, oversize material into the crusher, screening the content into the washing to take off the mud system.
  • 5. Resonance screen resonance screen is also called the elastic connecting rod type vibrating screen, large amplitude, high screening efficiency, processing capacity, small power consumption, compact structure. But the manufacturing process is complex, the machine quality, amplitude is stable, adjustment is more complex, rubber spring easy ageing.
  • 6. Linear vibrating screen, linear vibrating screen vibration amplitude, vibration, high screening efficiency, productivity, can sieve coarse piece of material. Due to horizontal installation, small installation height, straight line movement, back and forth on the dewatering, des and heavy media separation medium. But the structure is more complex, two axis of rotation speed is high, so the manufacturing and lubrication requirement is high, the vibration is not easy to adjust.
