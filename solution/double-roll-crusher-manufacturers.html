Double Roll Crusher Working

Different types of Double Roll Crusher technical parameters are also different. But the basic principle is substantially the same. The material is put several staggered Double Roll Crusher Roll on two impact crusher teeth, teeth that bite into the material is; If the material is not to be crushed, the material along the surface of the tooth Double Roll Crusher Roll on forced glide, by spiral arrangement of teeth, flip the material, waiting for the next successive tooth crushing, until the material can be crushing teeth roll bite so far. Roll bite into the material to be crushed, after the current one on the edge of the lower edge and the front teeth of a pair of teeth crushing, shearing action of the small particle size of the crushed material is discharged directly, large size materials were extrusion and extrusion force was cut, the material meets the requirements from the backlash leave out. Individual particle size larger block of material will be crushing stick, when crushing tooth movement when nearby bars, further shelved bulk material crushing between the rod and Roll chipping, and forcibly discharged.
