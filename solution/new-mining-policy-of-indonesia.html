New Mining Policy Of Indonesia

With the continuous progress of the construction of modern Indonesia, production demand is growing, it is necessary to use this new type of mining equipment. Although Indonesia is currently mining equipment and there is a gap compared to foreign countries, but as technology continues to mature, also began mining equipment exports, equipment quality has been highly appreciated by users.

Since Indonesia joined the WTO, many restrictions are canceled, for many construction industry in Indonesia has brought a new revitalization. In accordance with the specific requirements of the World Trade Organization, to do fewer restrictions, especially restrictions on exports of foreign trade, production, transportation and other industries also reduced, making mining equipment in Indonesia also exported to foreign countries. But for us, it still can not slack off, to do work -related mining equipment offer, coordinate investment and trade-related Policy.

Indonesia’s Trade Policy constantly relaxed, how clever use of technology to facilitate the process of foreign investment in Indonesia’s mining equipment has become the most critical thing. If foreign investment is still not in place, the proportion of small, then the development of mining equipment will bring great obstacles, making the scale of foreign investment and thus reduce the production of mining equipment exports to Indonesia in other countries, which for mining equipment and technology in Indonesia development is also a serious obstacle, the resulting loss of incalculable benefit.

Policy in Indonesia in recent years related to the introduction of foreign capital has also made ​​a detailed annotations, also strongly supports the introduction of foreign capital. However, exports of mining equipment is not a day or two things, requires the joint efforts of the whole society. The government of Indonesia to support the industry and the project is mainly high-tech industries, green eco- industry and high-tech industries, etc., and which for the Indonesian mining equipment put forward higher requirements, the need to improve the quality of the device itself, thereby bringing its overall production efficiency.
