Steel Slag Recycling

In today’s era of industrial take-off, the concept of sustainable development has permeated all aspects of production, life. Energy saving, environmental protection and some other words quietly into the public view, become a trend. In the steel industry, Steel Slag crushing equipment has such a wide range of land use trends, and achieved good results. Steel Slag is a blast furnace ironmaking, steelmaking process solid waste generated. Steelmaking slag discharged, according turn into furnace slag, flat slag, electric furnace slag. Discharge of about 15 to 20% of crude steel production. According to statistics, China has about 300 million tons Steel Slag, and also the number to 84 million tons of annual growth. If you press this trend, the number of Chinese Steel Slag future will be a shocking figure. Plenty of Steel Slag discharged as waste, not only occupy a lot of farmland and land, and caused serious environmental pollution.

Steel Slag Crushing Plant

In fact, Steel Slag contains a variety of useful resources, calcium, iron, silicon, magnesium, aluminum, manganese, phosphorus, iron and steel slag can be used in many fields. If instead of directly as a waste of resources, it is a huge waste of resources. In Steel Slag treatment process is mainly crushing. Steel Slag Crusher is based on experiments and research on the introduction of multiple, dedicated to Steel Slag crushing equipment. Machining processes are common, after Steel Slag crusher, then use the grinding equipment to be crushed Steel Slag grinding process, and finally dried by baking, magnetic screening, pellet manufacturing processes, access to four kind of efficient and high -quality value-added products, re- used for industrial production.
