Vibrating Screen Machine Brief Introduction

Vibrating Screen Machine is the use of a reciprocating rotary vibration Vibrating Screen Machine transducer excitation arising from the work. Vibrator on the screen surface to produce rotating weight makes the plane swing vibration, and the lower rotating weight makes Vibrating Screen Machine conical sieve surface to produce rotary vibration, the effect of their combined effect makes the screen surface to produce a reciprocating rotary vibration, so as to achieve sieving effect. Vibrating Screen Machine under the action of the vibrator, the vibration locus is a complicated space curves. This curve is a circular projection in a horizontal plane and in the vertical plane of projection is an oval.
